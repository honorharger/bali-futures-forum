About the Gathering

For the craftsman,
not the keynote

Join us in south Bali for an intimate, off-the-record unconference for futures and futures-adjacent practitioners. This is a space to share the director's cut of our work: what we're wrestling with, what's changing in our field and practice, and what it means to do this work well.

No media. No audience. Just curious minds, good food, and open-ended conversations under tropical skies.

Come for the conversation. Stay for the company.

What Is It

A gathering for
practitioners,
by practitioners

An off-the-record space to talk shop, show scars, and share the director's cut — not the press release version. The Bali Futures Forum is built around the conviction that the most valuable conversations in our field happen off-stage.

This is for the craftsman, not the crowd. A room small enough that every voice is heard, in a place with its own deep relationship to time, craft, and the future.
